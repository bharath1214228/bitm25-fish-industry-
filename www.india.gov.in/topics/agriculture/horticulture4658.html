  2. Website of Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH)

    • Share This

    Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme for the holistic development of horticulture in the country during XII plan. User can find the details of schemes, operational guidelines and contact details of MIDH officials.

  3. Website of Horticulture Mission for North East & Himalayan States (HMNEH)

    • Share This

    Horticulture Mission for North East & Himalayan States (HMNEH) is a part of Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture(MIDH) scheme, being implemented for overall development of Horticulture in NE and Himalayan states. The mission covers all NE States including Sikkim and three Himalayan states of Jammu & Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh and Uttarakhand. The mission addresses entire...

  4. State Department of Horticulture of Himachal Pradesh

    • Share This

    The State Department of Horticulture functions with the objective of building a prosperous Himachal through scientific development of horticulture by harnessing the natural resources for the development of a sustainable system of agriculture in the hilly areas. Users can get detailed information on fruit preservation, mushroom development, bee-keeping and plant nutritional.

  5. Website of HORTNET, Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers welfare

    • Share This

    The scheme, which has taken take off from 2014-15, integrates the ongoing schemes of National Horticulture Mission, Horticulture Mission for North East & Himalayan States, National Bamboo Mission, National Horticulture Board, Coconut Development Board and Central Institute for Horticulture, Nagaland.

  6. Information on horticulture of Tripura

    • Share This

    Information about the horticulture of Tripura is provided. Users can get information about production and productivity of fruits, fruits nut, vegetable and spices etc. Information about area of production of fruits, chilly, turmeric, and vegetable is also provided. Get details about processing infrastructure available in the state.

  7. Information on fruits cultivation by NHB

    • Share This

    Detailed information pertaining to fruits cultivation is provided by the National Horticulture Board (NHB). Users can find information related to apple cultivation, Banana cultivation, Citrus, Litchi and other fruits cultivation. Information such as area of cultivation, diseases, disorders, harvesting, planting, pests, etc. is provided.

  8. Website of Indian Plywood Industries Research and Training Institute

    • Share This

    The Indian Plywood Industries Research and Training Institute is a research and training institute that offers long term and short-term courses for students. Details related to divisions and facilities in the institute are provided. Information on research and training is provided. Details on accredited research institutes are available.

  9. Website of Indian Institute of Horticultural Research

    • Share This

    The Indian Institute of Horticultural Research is a premier Institute conducting basic, strategic, anticipatory and applied research on various aspects of fruits, vegetable, ornamental, medicinal and aromatic plants and mushrooms. Users can find information about soil and leaf analysis, pesticide residue analysis, technologies, varieties, agricultural operations, seed or planting material, etc...

  10. Website of National Horticulture Mission

    • Share This

    Users can find detailed information on the National Horticulture Mission. It was launched as a Centrally Sponsored Scheme to promote holistic growth of the horticulture sector through area based regionally differentiated strategies. Information is given on mission, events, progress monitoring, marketing, technology, etc. Revised norms and guidelines are also provided.
