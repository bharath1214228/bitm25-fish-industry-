  6. Information on Central Grain Analysis Laboratory

    • Share This

    Central Grain Analysis Laboratory (CGAL) is responsible for laying down quality standards for procurement of Kharif and Rabi food grains for Central Pool. Information about thrust areas, relaxation, training courses, etc. is given. Users can also access details related to Quality Control Cells (QCC) and Indian Grain Storage Management and Research Institute (IGMRI).

  7. Departments of Agriculture of states and Union Territories

    • Share This

    The Department of Agriculture and Cooperation has provided links for the states and Union Territories' Agriculture Departments. One needs to click on the name of the state or Union Territory to access the website of its agriculture department.

  8. Details of destilleries in Uttar Pradesh

    • Share This

    The Seven Distilleries are established to Produce rectified Spirit from the Molasses produced in Cooperative Sugar Mills. The details of their capacity and production in various districts of Uttar Pradesh are given.

  9. Find Minimum Support Prices of various agricultural products

    • Share This

    Find the Minimum Support Prices (MSPs) of various agricultural commodities recommended by the Commission for Agricultural Costs and Prices (CACP), Ministry of Agriculture. Users can get the prices of crops such as Paddy, Jowar, Bajra, Ragi, Moong, Urad, Wheat, Gram, Jute, Sugarcane, etc. Reco price and fixed price of each product is available from 2007-2008 to 2013-1014.

    The National Sugar Institute (NSI) in Kanpur under the Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food and Public Distribution aims at providing technical education and training in research in all branches of sugar.   12. Website of Directorate of Cashewnut & Cocoa Development

    • Share This

    The Directorate of Cashewnut and Cocoa Development is a national agency primarily engaged in the overall development of cashew and cocoa in India. Users can get detailed information about cashew and cocoa crops, schemes, programmes, implementing agencies, nurseries etc. Information about the publications of agency is also provided.

  13. Website of Tea Board of India

    • Share This

    The Tea Board of India is functioning as a statutory body of the Central Government under the Ministry of Commerce. Users can get detailed information about the tea policies, legislations, tea business schemes, tea research etc. You can also find statistics of tea prices. Information about the Darjeeling Tea is also given.

  14. Chandigarh State Agricultural Marketing Board/Market Committee

    • Share This

    The Chandigarh State Agricultural Marketing Board/Market Committee was established with an objective to control and supervise the marketing network of sale, purchase, storage and processing of processed or non processed agricultural produces. Users can get information about agricultural rates, prices, procurement and facilities available for farmers.
