Fisheries

  1. Website of Academic Management System (CIFE)

    • Share This

    Central Institute of Fisheries Education is the premier National Fisheries University of India dedicated to development of quality human resources in frontier areas of fisheries and aquaculture.

  2. Animal and Fisheries Resources Department of Bihar

    • Share This

    Get detailed information on the Animal and Fisheries Resources Department of Bihar. Information about policies, schemes, programmes, and latest activities of the department is given. Acts and Rules related to the animal and fisheries are available. Officials contact details are also provided.

  3. Information on System for Issuance of Fuel Permit Cards to Fishermen (SELF)

    • Share This

    System for Issuance of Fuel Permit Cards to Fishermen (SELF) is an work flow based integrated system, integrated with ReALCRAFt, FIMS , SAGARA to streamline the fuel card distribution to the registered vessel owners and fuel distribution to the valid fuel permit holders. And also providing total integrated end to end solution to the Fisherman.

  4. Website of Commissioner of Fisheries, Gujarat

    • Share This

    Gujarat state is at the top position in Fisheries as is at the west coast of India and it covers 1/5th part of India along the coast as well as it is exclusive economic zone.

  5. Website of Gujarat Fisheries Central Cooperative Association

    • Share This

    Gujarat Fisheries Central Co-operative Association Limited (GFCCA) is an apex cooperative body of the fishermen cooperatives in the State of Gujarat. It was established in the year 1956 with financial and administrative support of the Government of Gujarat.

  6. Find application form for obtaining permission to conduct breeding programme in private hatcheries in Gujarat

    • Share This

    Get the downloadable application form for obtaining permission to conduct breeding programme in private hatcheries in Gujarat. Users need to fill the personal and other information such as name, address, phone number, details of the fish breeding programme, marketing details, etc.

  7. Guidelines for fishing operations in Indian exclusive economic zone

    • Share This

    Get guidelines for fishing operations in Indian Exclusive Economic Zone (EEZ) by Department of Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries, Ministry of Agriculture. Guidelines for operation of Indian deep sea fishing vessels in Indian EEZ, format for submission of voyage data by Indian owned registered deep sea fishing vessels, preamble and order are also available.

  8. National Institute of Fisheries Post Harvest Technology and Training

    • Share This

    Get information about National Institute of Fisheries Post Harvest Technology and Training (NIFPHATT). Information about fish harvesting technology, post harvest technology, marine engineering, integrated fisheries project, etc. is given. Details related to training programmes are also available.

  9. Information about Fishery Department of Birbhum district

    • Share This

    Get information about the Fishery Department of Birbhum district in West Bengal. Users can get information about the fishery resources, private ownership, Beel and Baor etc. Users can get detailed information about the demand and production of fish seeds and table fish.

  10. Website of eServices for Department of Fisheries, West Bengal

    • Share This

    West Bengal is enriched with a significant wealth of Inland Fishery Resources as well as Marine Fishery Resources. The Government has taken many initiatives on formulating and implementing various schemes for uplifting livelihood of the fishing people and provided required infrastructure to make their life easy and also more fish production.

  11. Contact details of key officials of Fisheries Department of Tripura

    • Share This

    Get contact details of key officials of Department of Fisheries, Tripura. Users can get information about name of the official, designation and telephone numbers. Details of officials from the head office, West Tripura, South Tripura, North Tripura and Dhalai District are also available.

  12. Forms of Department of Animal Husbandry, Dairying & Fisheries

    • Share This

    Information about application forms of Department of Animal Husbandry, Dairying & Fisheries (DAHD) under the Ministry of Agriculture. Users can know about application forms for leave/extension of leave, import/export of livestock products for personal consumption & trading or marketing. Also, application for registration under Milk & Milk Product Order 1992 is available for...

  13. Website of West Bengal University of Animal & Fishery Sciences

    • Share This

    West Bengal University of Animal & Fishery Sciences was established on 1995 vide West Bengal University of Animal & Fishery Sciences Act - 1995 to impart education, training and to conduct research in veterinary and animal sciences, dairy sciences and fishery sciences and also to cater the needs of the farming community of the State of West Bengal.

  14. Form for change of particulars in certificate of registration of fishing vessel in Gujarat

    • Share This

    Find the form for change of particulars in the certificate of registration of fishing vessel in Gujarat. Users need to fill the personal and other information to complete the application.

  15. Format for Certificate of Registration of Fishing Vessel in Gujarat

    • Share This

    Download the format for certificate of registration of fishing vessel in Gujarat. Details of the owner, licence number, vessel type, length, etc. are available in the format. The issues authority should sign the certificate with date and office stamp.
